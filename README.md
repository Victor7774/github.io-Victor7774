# Project 3 

**Title:** AdventureWorks Sales Performance Dashboard **Power BI File:** [AdventureWorks Sales Performance Dashboard](https://github.com/YourUsername/YourRepository/blob/main/AdventureWorks%20Sales%20Dashboard.pbix) 

**Power BI Skills Used:** Data Transformation (Power Query): Imported, cleaned and transformed raw sales data by handling missing values, removing duplicates, standardising data types and preparing datasets for reporting. 
- Data Modelling: Designed a star schema by creating relationships between fact and dimension tables to improve query performance and ensure accurate analysis.
- DAX Measures & Calculations: Developed custom measures using DAX, including Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, Order Quantity, Average Order Value, Year-to-Date (YTD), Month-to-Date (MTD), Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth and Running Totals.
- Time Intelligence: Implemented advanced DAX time intelligence functions to analyse historical trends, seasonal performance and year-over-year comparisons. - KPI Development: Built business KPIs to monitor revenue, profitability, sales growth, customer performance and product performance against business objectives.
- Interactive Dashboard Design: Created an intuitive dashboard featuring KPI cards, bar charts, line charts, maps, treemaps, matrix tables and decomposition trees for executive reporting.
- Dashboard Interactivity: Implemented slicers, bookmarks, drill-through pages, tooltips and cross-filtering to provide users with an interactive analytical experience.
- Performance Optimisation: Optimised the data model and DAX calculations to improve dashboard responsiveness and report performance.
- Business Intelligence Reporting: Designed reports that support data-driven decision making by highlighting trends, top-performing products, customer segments and regional sales performance. 

**Project Description:** Developed an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution using Microsoft's AdventureWorks dataset to provide comprehensive insights into sales performance, customer behaviour and product profitability. The project involved importing and transforming data using Power Query, designing a scalable relational data model and creating advanced DAX measures to calculate key business metrics. Interactive dashboards were developed to monitor sales trends, revenue growth, profit margins, customer purchasing behaviour and product performance across multiple regions and time periods. The dashboard enables stakeholders to drill into specific business areas, compare historical performance, identify high-performing products and customer segments, and evaluate sales trends through dynamic filtering and visual storytelling. The solution demonstrates best practices in data modelling, dashboard design, business analytics and performance optimisation commonly used in modern Business Intelligence environments. 

**Key Dashboard Features:** Executive KPI Dashboard - Sales Performance Analysis - Customer Segmentation Analysis - Product Performance Dashboard - Regional Sales Analysis - Time Intelligence Reporting (YTD, MTD & YoY) - Dynamic Filtering and Drill-through Navigation - Interactive Tooltips and Cross-filtering - Profitability Analysis - Trend & Forecast Visualisations 

**Business Questions Answered:**  Which products generate the highest revenue and profit? - Which customer segments contribute the most sales? - How has revenue changed compared to previous years? - Which sales territories perform best? - What are the monthly and yearly sales trends? - Which product categories require improvement? - How do profit margins differ across products and regions? - Which customers generate the highest lifetime value? 

**Technology used:** Microsoft Power BI - Power Query - DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) - Data Modelling - Star Schema Design - Microsoft Excel (Data Source) - Business Intelligence & Data Visualisation
